Background – Russell Maxwell

Russell has worked in Banking and Finance for over 30 years, including Senior roles with St George Bank, Advance Leasing, BankSA and Beneficial Finance.

Positions held include State Lending Manager, State Operations Manager, Regional Manager QLD Advance Leasing and Senior Manager QLD St George Corporate and Business Banking.

Russell has a high level of experience and expertise in practically all forms of lending and is ideally positioned to address the needs of his clients. Russell can become your personal Relationship Manager for all of your borrowing requirements.

Why use Maxwell Financial Services?

Experience

• Russell has over 30 years experience in Banking and Finance including Senior Management positions in Corporate Banking, Equipment and Motor Vehicle finance and Residential Mortgage Lending

• Russell is tertiary qualified with a Diploma of Business (Accounting) and a Diploma of Financial Services.

Credibility

• Russell is a Full member of the Mortgage and Finance Association of Australia, having passed stringent compliance tests including Industry, Legislation and Product exams, Police name search, credit checking and referencing from other members.

• Russell has attended Product Training seminars for the major lenders and completed formal accreditation process for individual lenders where required

• Russell is a member of the Credit Ombudsman Service Limited, membership number 404056. This organisation is a professional, independent dispute resolution body which handles customer complaints.

Product Range

• Russell has accreditations with over 25 residential lenders providing access to over 2000 residential lending products. Our web based software program is updated daily providing up to date information on rates, fees and products.

• Russell has Commercial accreditations with the majority of commercial lenders which provides access to all commercial products including business acquisition and refinance, commercial property finance, equipment and motor vehicle finance and debtor funding.

Accessibility

• Our business is located at Spring Hill in the upper CBD. We have plenty of nearby parking and we are close Central Train Station. You are welcome to meet at our office or we can come to you.
